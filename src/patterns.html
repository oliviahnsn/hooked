patterns.

Patterns are tutorials (written or filmed) that you can follow to create crochet projects. There is a huge variety of both free and paid patterns available online. Here are some resources to help find the patterns you’re looking for:

pinterest

  • easily search for any type of pattern
  • compiles pattern links from around the internet into one place (with pictures!)
  • allows you to save interesting patterns for later use

youtube

  • great resource for free video patterns
  • useful for visual learners and beginners alike
  • some creators post pattern lists you can use to find even more tutorials across the internet

ravelry

  • largest crochet and knit database
  • free and paid patterns made by other site users
  • free account needed to access content

yarn brands

  • includes sites on “supplies” tab - yarnspirations, hobbii, lovecrafts
  • free and paid patterns available
  • sometimes include option to buy the needed yarn straight from pattern page

etsy

  • paid patterns only
  • many unique and complex patterns available
  • always be sure you’re buying the pattern and not a finished piece (and vice versa)
